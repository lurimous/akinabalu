Our Story

Flavor Rooted
in Heritage

Named after the legendary peaks of Mount Kinabalu, Aki-Nabalu Fried Chicken was founded on a simple promise — serve food that's as bold and unforgettable as the mountains that inspired us.

Every piece of chicken is hand-marinated with our signature spice blend, coated in a perfectly seasoned crispy crust, and cooked fresh to order. No shortcuts. No compromise. Just great chicken.
